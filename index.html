My Journey & Experience

My professional journey has been a dynamic and purposeful one. It all began with the founding of Veeery, where I set out to provide seamless learning experiences to visually impaired individuals. This startup not only allowed me to explore entrepreneurship but also fostered my skills in product management and leadership. In 2022, I made the decision to transition Veeery's projects into non-profit initiatives under Dharnika, my NGO, furthering our mission of promoting inclusive education and employment generation for Divyaangjan.

In July 2022, I embarked on a new chapter with Smartivity, where I led successful product launches and played a pivotal role in enhancing user experiences. However, in December 2022, I transitioned to Mahindra to continue driving impactful change in the product management space.

At Mahindra, I've been instrumental in streamlining processes across the used car business, integrating payment solutions, optimizing report management, introducing vendor management platforms, and revamping inventory management systems.
