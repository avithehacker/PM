Overview

What is BuyerPulse?

BuyerPulse is an internal auction operations dashboard for buyer success teams managing large dealer portfolios in vehicle auction platforms. It consolidates buyer health monitoring, risk management, follow-up task tracking, analytics, and AI-generated summaries into a single interface.

Problem Statement

Why BuyerPulse?

Auction operations teams managing hundreds of active dealer-buyers face compounding, disconnected problems that slow down proactive management.

01
Fragmented Data
Buyer health, EMD balances, bid history, and follow-up tasks live across separate tools — CRM, spreadsheets, and auction software.
02
Reactive Risk Management
Teams only identify at-risk buyers after defaults or no-shows — not before. No early-warning system exists.
03
Manual Follow-Up Tracking
Call logs and task assignments are managed via email threads or shared sheets, with no accountability or due-date visibility.
04
No Unified Portfolio View
Managers lack a live dashboard to understand where attention is needed right now across the full buyer base.

Features

Platform Modules

Eight interconnected modules — each solving a discrete ops workflow.

Module 01
Dashboard
Live KPI stat cards, portfolio health bar, auction status, pending follow-ups, and quick action buttons. Morning briefing at a glance.
Module 02
Buyers
Paginated buyer roster with live search, risk filter pills, and a slide-in detail drawer with 5 tabs: Details, Activity, Follow Ups, Payments, Notes.
Module 03
Health Scores
Portfolio-wide health scoring — score distribution histogram, portfolio snapshot, individual buyer health cards with gauge and filter row.
Module 04
Risk Center
Triage view for flagged buyers. Risk feed cards with reason, EMD exposure, and action buttons: Contact, Suspend, Resolve. Bulk resolve for Critical tier.
Module 05
Follow Ups
Three-column kanban (To Do → In Progress → Done). Task cards with type, priority, due date, and assignee. Add Follow Up modal with full form.
Module 06
Notifications
Bell dropdown with unread badge, tabbed notification page (All / Unread / Alerts / Updates), Mark All Read, and View All actions.
Module 07
Analytics
Pure CSS/SVG charts — health trend line chart, risk distribution donut, auction activity bar chart, and top buyers table. 7D / 30D / 90D date filter.
Module 08
AI Insights
AI-generated operational summaries with thinking overlay animation, character-by-character typing effect, expand/collapse cards, and 3-variant rotation.

Module Detail

AI Insights

A simulated AI operations layer — typing animation, thinking overlay, and structured summaries — that communicates the intended product experience in demos without a live model.

How It Works

StepWhat Happens
1. GenerateUser clicks "Generate Summary". The button disables and the thinking overlay activates.
2. ThinkingAnimated three-dot loader with "Analyzing portfolio data…" label plays for 1.8 seconds.
3. RenderSix insight cards slide in one by one, each starting the typing animation immediately.
4. TypingText types character-by-character at ~11ms per char, with longer pauses at punctuation (55ms at ".", 28ms at ",").
5. Expand / CollapseEach card has a toggle to show full detail or collapse to a one-line summary.
6. VariantsThree summary variants rotate on each generation for demo freshness.

Design Decision

AI as UX Metaphor

The AI Insights page is not connected to any language model. The typing animation and thinking overlay create the feel of AI reasoning — communicating product intent and user experience in a demo context without requiring infrastructure. This is a deliberate prototyping technique: the interaction design validates the concept independently of the ML layer.

Design Principles

PrincipleImplementation
No frameworkPlain HTML/CSS/JS — zero build step, instant GitHub Pages deploy, shareable as a link
Deterministic dataAll extended data derives from buyer.id via modular arithmetic — no Math.random() in data generation
CSS variablesAll colours, shadows, spacing in :root — enables full theming with a single class swap
Accessible markupARIA roles, aria-hidden, aria-expanded, aria-modal on drawers and dropdowns
No inline style mutationsRipple clipping via CSS overflow: hidden, not JS, preventing permanent side-effects
